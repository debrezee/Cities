London

London is the largest city in Europe and located astride the famous river of Thames. This is the home for most of the international organizations and the city is headed by the Lord Mayor of London. The city of London has its own police force, call City of London police.The city of London is consists of different sub areas and they have separate postal code for each. Many events taken place in London annually and city provides a good transport system also. London underground is convenient and cheap way of traveling. London is the selected venue for year 2012 Olympics and infrastructure work already started for that.
